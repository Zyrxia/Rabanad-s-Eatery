Rabanadz Eatery was established with a simple goal: to provide affordable, home-cooked meals that bring comfort to everyday life. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we opened our first small shop in Matina, driven by the desire to serve our community during challenging times.

We are a small team that values kindness, respect, and good service. At Rabanadz Eatery, we treat our customers like family and always strive to create a warm and welcoming experience. As things began to improve after the pandemic, we decided to continue and grow our journey by relocating to Obrero, Lupo Diaz. With a better location near schools, workplaces, and busy areas, we were able to reach more customers and share our food with more people.

Today, Rabanadz Eatery continues to serve delicious and budget-friendly meals, staying true to our roots of providing warm, satisfying food that feels like home.
